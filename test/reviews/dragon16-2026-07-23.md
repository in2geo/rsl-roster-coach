## What this run tells the simulator (survival is the gap)

- **The `taken` distribution is the signature of working protection.** Hits pile onto the **tank (Pelops
  23,205)** and the **damage sponge (Vergis 45,610)**; the **healer (Tagoar) takes only 5,778** and
  **Ezio is untouchable behind Perfect Veil (3,820)**. The team takes ~88k total over 155 turns and heals
  ~232k — massively net positive.
- **The sim is the exact inverse:** it lets a mob hit **Tagoar for 15,803** and kills him in wave 1. So the
  survival gap is **not "not enough healing"** — it is that the sim **doesn't keep the hits off the
  protected champions.** Fix that and the healing already present out-paces the incoming.
- **Verified sim divergences (2026-07-23 session, engine at commit 07c5899):**
  - Offense now matches in shape — Ezio opens AoE, mobs chunked early, HP-Burn grind (fixed this session).
  - **Survival mechanics the sim does NOT fire (the build backlog):** Vergis **Second Wind** (Shield +
    Continuous Heal passive, `no-trigger`), Tagoar **Aid the Feeble** (−10% dmg to allies ≤50% HP), Pelops
    **−20% team damage reduction** (A3 passive). Plus **shield uptime** (Tagoar unshielded t6→27; Magma
    Shield fires t4 then t37) and **Ally Protection** not redistributing the big hits.
  - Boss-phase mechanics unverifiable until survival is fixed: **poison explosion** (Ezio ~t124, unmodelled)
    and the **Magma reflect off the Dragon** (~t153, modelled — should fire once the team reaches R3).
