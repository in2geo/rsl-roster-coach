What we collect

When you upload a roster screenshot, the image is sent to our server solely to identify your champions. We do not store the image after processing. We store the structured champion data (name, level, star rank) linked to an anonymous session ID.

The app uses rewarded video ads. The ad network may collect device identifiers in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not pass personally identifiable information to ad networks.

We use browser local storage to remember your daily recommendation limit. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.
