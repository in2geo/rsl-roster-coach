## 3. What was MEASURED (the durable science)

**⚠ Grades before and after the build floor are NOT comparable** — the earlier numbers were inflated
by ungeared champions "filling" buckets.

### 3.1 The Glorious Pallas synergy — QUANTIFIED, replicated
Clan Boss Stage 13 (= Brutal), Don$Gnut, three runs differing by ONE champion:

| | 5th slot | Pelops/turn | turns | total |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A | Ezio + **Pallas** | **38.4k** | 217 | 19.99M |
| B | Ezio + Gnut *(no Pallas)* | **28.6k** | 189 | 18.40M |
| C | Gnut + **Pallas** *(no Ezio)* | **37.3k** | 217 | 19.73M |

**Pelops holds ~37-38k/turn WITH Pallas and collapses to 28.6k WITHOUT her, regardless of the other
slot.** Her granted damage ≈ **9-10k/turn**, ~10× her own damage bar. Mechanism is already ruled:
`lib/synergies.js` `pallas_argonite` — her A1 pulls a random **Argonites** ally (Pelops) into the
attack for free, and his mastery procs on it. All of it lands in HIS bar.
**This is the first quantified granted-damage anchor for the contribution model.**

### 3.2 Affinity may be FIRST-ORDER on clear time
Don$Gnut Fire Knight, same five champions:

| stage | boss affinity | Ezio (the ATK carrier, Spirit) | turns |
|---|---|---|---|
| 13 | Force | **STRONG** | **96** |
| 16 | Void | neutral | 353 |

until this is modelled.

### 3.3 A WIN can be a FAILURE — and the harness can't see it
FK 15 was a **victory in 565 turns / 26.4 minutes**, all five alive. `CLAUDE.md` judges auto content
by TIME (~5 min reference). `shadow-grade-clears` scored it as a clear. The FK rubric graded that team
**106.7 — its highest of all five contents** — on the path literally named *"Survive grind"*, with
`survive` filled to **190%**. It optimised for survival, got survival, and couldn't kill.
**Over-supply of a non-binding constraint is the recurring defect** (see also the CB RES-lead pick).

### 3.6 Gear corpus — the tier definition
- 723 champions not in storage; **627 have NO gear at all**; 96 geared; **31 are stage-20 carriers**.
- `gearTierFromArtifacts` (rank/level thresholds) is **nearly uncorrelated** with gear contribution:
  a "Starter" reached 4.06×, a "God Tier" sat at 1.25×, and "Dungeon" classified 8 of 897.
- **Mike's ruling: the tier must be tied to PROGRESSION, not to a distribution percentile** —
  "good" = clears dungeon 20 + Brutal CB two-key; "fair" = clears dungeon 10.
  Percentiles were rejected because they anchor "good" to whatever the synced rosters happen to hold.
- **Mike's benchmark, validated against the carriers:** attackers near 100% crit + some crit damage;
  speed at the content requirement; **ACC close to the gate but often not there**. Measured carrier
  attackers: **crit 89 · crit dmg 113 · ACC 142** (vs a 170-225 gate — "close but not there", exactly).
- **Role-aware by ruling:** supports are built HP/DEF not ATK/crit (confirmed: Attack ATK 1909/CRATE 78
  vs Support ATK 1092/DEF 1300/CRATE 20). Debuffers chase the ACC cap first, then HP/DEF.
  ⚠ "is a debuffer" as a champion-level boolean is USELESS — 80 of 96 carry some debuff tag. The
  bucket scorer's per-TAG ACC gate is the right instrument.

### 3.7 The declared stat floors are FALSIFIED
Of 96 geared champions across six accounts: **ZERO** meet Dragon stage-10's `ACC 150 + RES 250`, and
**ZERO** exceed RES 250 at all (max 214) — yet Dragon 20 is cleared routinely. Observed gear on 827
winning hero-rows at stage 20+: **HP ~30k, ACC ~117, RES ~111**, vs declared ACC 200-225 / RES 300.
**RES barely moves across the entire progression** (98→100→112→111) — it is gating nothing.
ACC and HP are the stats that actually track progression. This is "floors are not gates", quantified.
(⚠ RES is also inflated by §4.1, so real RES is LOWER still.)

### 4.1 ⚠⚠ TWO STAT-ID MAPS, DIFFERENT ID SPACES, BOTH WRONG — **blocks all stat work**
`sync.js STAT_KIND` (artifact main/sub stats) and `lib/effective-stats.js STAT_KIND_ID` (`bonusesV2`
statKindId) **disagree on 5 of 8 shared IDs** and are demonstrably two different enumerations.

PROVEN by slot restrictions (C.RATE/C.DMG = Gauntlets only, ACC/RES = Chestplate only, SPD = Boots only):
- **artifact-space `id7` = SPD**, currently `RES` — it appears on **boots only, 70 of 81**, and Mike

  applies **"+12 res"**; her crit rate stays at base.

  Weapons = ATK) and `id8` as `acc` though it appears only on Gauntlets where ACC cannot roll.

**CONSEQUENCE: RES is inflated, SPD and CRIT are under-counted, ACC is uncertain — on every Gestal
champion.** Every ACC/RES/SPD/crit number in this document must be re-derived after the fix,
including "Narma ACC 112 vs the 150 floor" and the §3.6 carrier benchmark.
