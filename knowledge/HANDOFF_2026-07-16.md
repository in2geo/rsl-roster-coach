### 2. Content problem-models — `lib/dungeon-mechanics.js`  → memory [[dungeon-mechanics-model-2026-07-16]]
- **THE TARGET IS ALWAYS A ONE-KEY CLEAR of the level attempted.** It's the build: `match-engine.scanDungeonStages` returns the highest stage the roster can clear.

### 4. Clan Boss feedback loop  → memory [[cb-feedback-wipe-turn-2026-07-16]]
- **Target** = top-chest damage threshold per difficulty (VERIFIED in `clan_boss_chest_tiers`): Easy 1.15M · Normal 3.64M · Hard 11.65M · Brutal 21.7M · Nightmare 39.17M · UNM 70.28M.

- **PREREQUISITE (reader work):** the metric is the **BOSS TURN COUNT** at wipe, NOT total combat `turns` (total is confounded by team speed). The reader must isolate the boss's own turn count. Until then, wipes can't be placed on the Gathering-Fury ramp (10/20/50).
