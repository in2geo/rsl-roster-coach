## Traps (this session's)

- **The reader silently drops a hero it can't validate** (roster staleness / >255 heroId / boss junk).
  A 4-hero CB/Dragon capture where you expect 5 is a CAPTURE miss, not a real result — don't grade it.
- **"Google AI Overview" `source_citation` = fabricated data.** 3 fake champions came from it.
- **The word-run phantom scan is WRONG on the 4 starters** (Kael/Elhain/Athel/Galek) and any
  apostrophe/prepositional name. Discriminator is PAYLOAD (0 skills/tags/auras/stats), not name shape.
- **Grids and the worksheet are a POINT-IN-TIME snapshot** — they can't find champions newer than
  themselves (Aria/Xanthe/Vallaryn). "In DB not in grid" = fake OR new; disambiguate by source+payload.
- **CB is scored by DAMAGE vs the top-chest threshold of the difficulty run, never by kill/floor.**
  Difficulty is captured (stageId 4019xxx encodes it → `lib/clan-boss.js`), NOT a reader gap.
- **`champion_tags` has `source_note`, not `review_notes`** (bit seed 166).
- **The engine reads `status='approved'` tags live** — a committed-but-unapplied rejection seed means
  it still over-credits. (0 proposed now; all approved/rejected.)
- **repo is PUBLIC + committed to `main`, NOT pushed.** Don't push unless asked.
