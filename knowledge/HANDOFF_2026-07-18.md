## Traps (this session's)

- **PostgREST hard-caps responses at 1000 rows regardless of the `Range` header.** A `Range:
  0-1999` returns 1000 and `content-range: 0-999/*`. This silently undercounted a cross-check by
  21 champions and is the reason the old handoff's crit split (487/513) is wrong. **Always page.**
- **My word-run "duplicate" heuristic has ~16% false positives, including all four starter
  champions.** Search tool only. See the ruling section.
- **`download_file_content` returns base64**, not text, even for `text/csv`.
- **Don't use heredocs for JS containing regex escapes** in the Bash tool — `\\s` and `\\$&` get
  eaten and you get "Invalid regular expression". Use the Write tool.
- **The stat worklist cannot see missing champions.** It enumerates rows; a champion with no row
  is invisible to it. Do not treat "gap = 39" as "39 champions from complete".
