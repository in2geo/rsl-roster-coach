  handoff's crit split (487/513) is wrong. **Always page.**

  only.

- **`download_file_content` returns base64**, even for `text/csv`.
- **Don't use heredocs for JS with regex escapes** in the Bash tool — `\\s` gets eaten.
- **The stat worklist cannot see missing champions.** "1 gap left" ≠ "1 champion from complete".
